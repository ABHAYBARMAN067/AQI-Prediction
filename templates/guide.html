How to Use This AQI Prediction App

  1. Enter Pollution Data: PM2.5, PM10, NO2, SO2, CO, and O3 values in the form above.
  2. Submit the Form: Click the Predict AQI button to get the air quality index.
  3. View Result: The predicted AQI will be displayed with color-coded health information.
  4. Take Precautions: Follow the tips provided to protect yourself on days with poor air quality.
  5. Learn More: Explore the sections below to understand AQI levels and how to control air pollution.

Pollutants Defined

  • PM2.5: Tiny particles 2.5 micrometers or smaller that can penetrate deep into lungs.
  • PM10: Larger particles up to 10 micrometers causing irritation to eyes, nose, and throat.
  • NO2: Nitrogen dioxide gas from vehicles and industry, causing respiratory issues.
  • SO2: Sulfur dioxide gas from burning fossil fuels, harmful to lungs.
  • CO: Carbon monoxide gas that reduces oxygen in the blood and can be dangerous.
  • O3: Ground-level ozone gas causing chest pain and breathing problems.
